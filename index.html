THE FLOOR ONLY RISES.

PulseVerify is free, open, and serverless — built and given away by PulseStrategy, a family of ownerless vault tokens designed around one rule: the amount of backing behind each token can never go down. Not "shouldn't." Cannot.
  • No owner. No admin keys. No pause switch, no upgrades, no governance — the rules can never change.
  • The floor ratchets up. Every mint adds more backing than tokens; every redemption leaves value behind.
  • 100% publicly minted. Zero team allocation, zero pre-mine, zero emissions.
  • Holders collect the churn. Every round trip through a vault leaves ~4.3% behind for people who simply hold.
